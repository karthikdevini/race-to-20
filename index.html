About the Game

Race to 20 is a thrilling two-player strategy game. Each player adds either 1 or 2 to a shared total on their turn. The player who lands exactly on 20 wins.

  • Players alternate turns.
  • Only +1 or +2 is allowed per turn.
  • Going over 20 means instant loss.
  • The goal: reach 20 exactly!
  • Track moves in real-time history (latest on top).
